牛津 3000 词学习手册 (Unit 1)

练习内容#

1#

(meaning) a thing that somebody says or writes giving their opinion or stating a fact

某人说或写的东西，发表他们的意见或陈述一个事实

  • A. point(观点)
  • B. peak
  • C. description

2#

(meaning) the main or most important idea in something that is said or done

所说或所做的事情的主要或最重要的想法

  • A. tip
  • B. point(观点)
  • C. cap

3#

(meaning) the purpose or aim of something

某事的目标或目的

  • A. want
  • B. need
  • C. point(意图)

4#

(meaning) to stretch out your finger or something held in your hand towards somebody/something in order to show somebody where a person or thing is

向某人/某物伸出您的手指或手中的某物，以向某人显示某人或某物所在的位置

  • A. point(指向)
  • B. pass
  • C. lead

5#

(meaning) to aim something at somebody/something

瞄准某人/某事

  • A. follow
  • B. point(指向)
  • C. reach

6#

(meaning) to face in or be directed towards a particular direction

面对或被指向一个特定的方向

  • A. front
  • B. lead
  • C. point(指向)

7#

(meaning) having a sharp end

有一个尖头

  • A. pointed(尖)
  • B. steep
  • C. rough

8#

(meaning) to mention something in order to give somebody information about it or make them notice it

提及某事以便向某人提供有关它的信息或使他们注意到它

  • A. play out
  • B. make out
  • C. point out(指出)

9#

(usage) I should point ___ that not one of these paintings is original.

我应该指出，这些画中没有一幅是原创的。

  • A. by
  • B. out
  • C. clear
note

8 解释 point out

10#

(usage) OK, you've ___ your point!

好吧，你已经提出了你的观点！你的观点是什么？

  • A. taken
  • B. done
  • C. made
note

1 解释 point

make a point 提出一个观点

11#

(usage) She pointed ___ my direction.

她指着我的方向。

  • A. in
  • B. at
  • C. for
note

4 解释 point

point in 指着

12#

(usage) He has bushy eyebrows and a ___ chin.

他有浓密的眉毛和尖尖的下巴。

  • A. pointing
  • B. pointed
  • C. point
note

7 解释 pointed

13#

(meaning) a group of people who play a particular game or sport against another group of people

一群人与另一群人玩特定的游戏或运动

  • A. team(团队)
  • B. gang
  • C. match

14#

(meaning) a group of people who work together at a particular job

在特定工作中一起工作的一群人

  • A. match
  • B. team
  • C. club

15#

(usage) ___ team are you in?

你在谁的团队？

  • A. Who's
  • B. Whose(谁的)
  • C. Whos
note
  • whose 是代词 who 的所有格形式
  • who's 是单词 who is 或 who has 的缩写。

16#

(meaning) to prevent something bad from happening

防止坏事发生

  • A. remove
  • B. invert
  • C. avoid

17#

(meaning) to keep away from somebody/something; to try not to do something

远离某人/某事；尽量不做某事

  • A. ignore
  • B. run
  • C. avoid

18#

(usage) They narrowly avoided defeat.

他们勉强避免了失败。

  • A. narrowly(勉强)
  • B. thinly
  • C. leanly
note

narrowly (adverb): in a way that is limited 以一种有限的方式

19#

(meaning) a female person or animal that has already been mentioned or is easily identified

已经被提及或容易识别的女性人或动物

  • A. he
  • B. they
  • C. she

20#

(usage) ___ she look like Sue

  • A.Don't
  • B. Doesn't(单数用 does)
  • C. Isn't
