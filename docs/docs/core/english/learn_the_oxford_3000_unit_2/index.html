牛津 3000 词学习手册 (Unit 2)

练习内容#

1#

(meaning) a small stick made of wood or cardboard that is used for lighting a fire, cigarette, etc

用木头或纸板制成的小棍子，用于点燃火、香烟等

  • A. match(火柴)
  • B. lighter
  • C. candle

2#

(meaning) a sports event where people or teams compete against each other

个人或团队相互竞争的体育赛事

  • A. league
  • B. round
  • C. match(比赛)

3#

(meaning) a person or thing that combines well with somebody/something else

与某人/其他事物很好地结合的人或事物

  • A. equivalent
  • B. match(般配)
  • C. associate

4#

(meaning) to find somebody/something that goes together with or is connected with another person or thing

找到与另一个人或事物相伴或相关的某人/某物

  • A. match(匹配)
  • B. meet
  • C. separate

5#

(meaning) to be the same or similar

相同或相似

  • A. match up(符合)
  • B. meet up
  • C. join up

6#

(meaning) to find things that belong together or that look attractive together

找到属于一起或看起来很有吸引力的东西

  • A. fix sth up
  • B. match sth up(配对)
  • C. mix sth up

7#

(meaning) having the same colour, pattern, style, etc. and therefore looking attractive together

具有相同的颜色、图案、风格等，因此在一起看起来很有吸引力

  • A. joint
  • B. fitting
  • C. matching(搭配)

8#

(usage) We bought a table with four ___ chairs

我们买了一张桌子和四把相配的椅子

  • A. matching
  • B. matches
  • C. match(相配)

9#

(usage) Jo and Ian are a ___ match for each other.

乔和伊恩是绝配。

  • A. prime
  • B. perfect(完美相配)
  • C. utter
note

3 解释 match

10#

(usage) The dark clouds matched ___.

她的心情犹如乌云一样灰暗。

  • A. for her mood
  • B. to her mood
  • C. her mood(符合心情)

11#

(meaning) an object whose name you do not use because you do not need to or want to, or because you do not know it

一个你不使用其名称的对象，因为你不需要或不想使用它，或者因为你不知道它

  • A. thing(指代事物)
  • B. product
  • C. type

12#

(meaning) an object that is not alive in the way that people and plants are

一个不像人和植物那样有生命力的物体

  • A. creature
  • B. being
  • C. thing(物体)

13#

(meaning) objects, clothing or tools that belong to somebody or are used for a particular purpose

属于某人或用于特定目的的物品、衣服或工具

  • A. bits
  • B. things(用品)
  • C. luggage
